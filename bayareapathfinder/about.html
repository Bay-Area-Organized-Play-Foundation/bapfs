Bay Area Pathfinder Society

Stay Informed!

There are two primary means to stay up-to-date with the current Pathfinder events in the San Francisco Bay Area- the mailing list and our Facebook page. Both of these are active and contain questions, answers, events, and ways you can get more involved too! In addition, we also have our Meetup group you can join to hear about the great events we help run.

Updating Information on the Site

If you have a new location or an update to any information on this site, please send an email to the mailing list, a message on Facebook, or fix it up directly on the site's Github repository.
