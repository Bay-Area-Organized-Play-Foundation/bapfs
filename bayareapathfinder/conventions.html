Bay Area Pathfinder Society

Conventions

Although a weekly game is a great way to get to know other players in your city, there is something different about spending a few days telling a longer story through modules or special events. To participate in one of these, take a look at the conventions we help organize throughout the year!

DunDraCon

DunDraCon kicks off the year on February 17th to 20th, 2017 at the San Ramon Marriott Hotel.

ConQuest Sacramento

ConQuest SAC will be held April 7-9, 2017 at the Crowne Plaza Sacramento. It is Sacramento's largest and oldest convention!

KublaCon

KublaCon is our biggest convention, held over Memorial Day weekend. Our event in 2017 will take place May 26th to 29th at the Hyatt Regency SFO.

PaizoCon

PaizoCon, one of the best Pathfinder-focused conventions (since it is hosted by Paizo themselves!) will also take place from May 26th to 29th, 2017 in Seattle, WA.

Stockton Con

StocktonCon fills up the Stockton Arena over August 19th and 20th 2017.

Gen Con

Gen Con celebrates its 50th year in Indianapolis, comprising four days from August 17th to 20th 2017. It is one of the most famous gaming conventions, and many hope to play Pathfinder Society in the massive Sagamore Ballroom at least once!

Pacificon

Pacificon Game Expo is the best way to spend your Labor Day weekend, running September 1st through 4th at the Santa Clara Marriott Hotel.

Big Bad Con

Big Bad Con doesn't have too much Pathfinder Society, but this is actually a great opportunity to try all kinds of other games! This year's convention is October 14th to 16th at the Walnut Creek Marriott.
