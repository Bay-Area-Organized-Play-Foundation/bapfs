Welcome to the Bay Area Pathfinder Society.

The Bay Area Pathfinder Society exists to bring the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game through Pathfinder Society gaming to the San Francisco Bay Area region. If you would like to be a store coordinator, judge, or player, please contact us today and we will help!

Please note the stores and game times as well as the links and convention listings. Don't forget to sign up with the newsgroup! It's how we do most of our regular communication.
