Conventions & Events

There is always something going on somewhere and we are oblivious to most of it. :) Other than the store events (listed on the left generally) we will try to keep an updated list of goings on in our neighborhood, and 'nearby' conventions. As always, if we have missed something let us know so we can add it by emailing Euan.

DunDraCon
DunDraCon is held in February at the San Ramon Marriott Hotel.

SacConquest
SacConquest is held at the end of March at the Sacramento Red Lion Hotel in Sacramento.

ConQuest Sac VI
ConQuest Sac is held in April at the Sacramento Marriott Rancho Cordova.

KublaCon
KublaCon is held in May at the Burlingame Hyatt Regency.

Paizo Con
Paizo Con is held in May in Seattle, WA.

Stockton Con
Stockton Con is held in early August in Stockton, CA.

Gen Con
Gen Con is held in August in Indianapolis, IN.

CelestiCon
CelestiCon Game Convention is held in August at the Redwood City Hotel Sofitel.

Pacificon
Pacificon Game Expo is held in August at the Santa Clara Marriott Hotel.

Big Bad Con
Big Bad Con is held in October at the Hilton Oakland Airport.
