Games

Pathfinder and Starfinder are very popular in the Bay Area, and our community is diverse and welcoming. You can join the Oakland & San Francisco Pathfinder Society Discussion Group on Facebook to ask questions and get to know your fellow tabletop players! You can also join the Discord Group, Slack Group, or the mailing list for Bay Area Pathfinder Society.
