# Organized Play During Coronavirus

We've moved all events to the virtual tabletop environment until further notice.

As many of you know, coronavirus is becoming a serious issue: business are asking employees to work from home where possible, public sporting events are being canceled, and schools are closing.

The virus appears to be transmissible while carriers are pre- and post-symptomatic; this means that it is not possible to gauge whether you are a risk to others.

To keep our community safe and comfortable, we are canceling all public, in-person events for the remainder of March. We will follow up with our communities on the 25th regarding games in the first and second weeks of April — so stay tuned. (If you are not on our Slack or Discord channels yet, this is a great opportunity to sign up and coordinate some Pathfinder online!)

As always, please let us know if you have any questions.
